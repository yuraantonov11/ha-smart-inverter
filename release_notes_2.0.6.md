## Smart Inverter App v2.0.6

### Highlights
- Improved Dashboard layout: the energy flow diagram is now shown as a dedicated top section for faster status checks.
- Simplified navigation shell: removed redundant page header titles and kept status visibility focused on realtime state.
- Expanded hardware settings with grid input breaker, nominal voltage, house reserve load, and AUTO reserve mode.
- Added safe charge estimate block with calculated charge power/current limits and ETA to 100% based on current SOC.
- Refreshed EN/UK localization strings for new settings and diagnostics labels.
