# Smart Solar Inverter

Monitor and control your solar inverter with battery storage directly from Home Assistant. HEMS modes, self-learning solar forecast, and full control — zero external dependencies.

## 🔌 Zero-config solar forecast

No panel specs needed. The self-learning algorithm uses your actual PV data + built-in sun position + existing weather entity to predict generation — automatically accounting for shading, tilt, and degradation.

## 🇺🇦 Ukrainian localization

Full Ukrainian translation of all entities, states, and configuration UI.
